# 🎮 Gesture-Controlled Android Game

An Android game controlled entirely by **hand gestures** using your device's camera — no touch needed!

---

## 🕹️ About the Game

You play as a character running along an **infinite road** filled with monsters and obstacles. Survive as long as possible and rack up your score by defeating monsters — all using hand gestures captured live through your camera!

---

## 🖐️ Gesture Controls

| Gesture | Action | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 🖐️ **Open Palm** | Block | Shields you from enemy bullets |
| ✊ **Fist** | Shoot Laser | Kills monsters & increases score |
| ✌️ **Peace Sign** | Jump | Avoid obstacles on the road |

---

## 🎯 How to Play

- Your character runs automatically on an **infinite road**
- Monsters appear and **shoot bullets** at you
- Show your **open palm** 🖐️ to the camera to **block incoming bullets**
- Make a **fist** ✊ to **shoot a laser** and kill monsters
- Flash a **peace sign** ✌️ to **jump** over obstacles
- Every monster you kill adds to your **score**
- The game ends when you get hit by an unblocked bullet or obstacle!

---

## 📊 Scoring

- Each monster killed = **+1 Score**
- How many monsters can you defeat? 🏆

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Android device with a front/rear camera
- Android 7.0 (Nougat) or higher
- Good lighting for accurate gesture detection

### Installation
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/Dixitjoy08/Gesture-Controlled-Android-Game.git
   ```
2. Open the project in **Android Studio**
3. Build and run the app on your Android device

---

## 💡 Tips for Best Experience

- Make sure your hand is **clearly visible** to the camera
- Play in a **well-lit** environment for accurate gesture detection
- Keep your gestures **clear and deliberate** for best recognition
